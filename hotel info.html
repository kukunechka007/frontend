Hotel Samanala Sigiriya is a guest house in Sigiriya, 2.8 km and 6 km from Sigiriya Plateau and Pidurangala Rock, respectively. It offers a garden, free Wi-Fi and free private parking. This property is 1.2 km and 2.1 km from Wildlife Range Office - Sigiriya and Sigiriya Museum, respectively.

The Forgotten Temple Kaludiya Pokuna is 10 km away. The guest house offers an indoor pool and food and beverage service. Rooms at Hotel Samanala Sigiriya have a seating area. Each room has a private bathroom with a bidet, free toiletries. Bed linen and towels are provided at Hotel Samanala Sigiriya.

A continental breakfast is served daily at Hotel Samanala Sigiriya.
he reception staff at Hotel Samanala Sigiriya are always on hand to provide you with the information you need.
